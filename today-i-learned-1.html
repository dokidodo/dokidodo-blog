Today I Learned - 1

I doubt I'll do this with everything, but I'd like to start recording some of the Japanese I learn, as I learn it. For today, that means kanji.

 

氷 - ice     こおり (kun'yomi) "koori"
他 - other     た (on'yomi) "ta"
休 - rest     きゅう (on'yomi) "kyuu"
先 - previous     せん (on'yomi) "sen"
世 - world     せい (on'yomi) "sei"
字 - letter     じ (on'yomi) "ji"
主 - master     しゅ (on'yomi) "shu"
年 - year     ねん (on'yomi) "nen"
名 - name     めい (on'yomi) "mei"
皿 - plate     さら (kun'yomi) "sara"
写 - copy     しゃ (on'yomi) "sha"
申 - say humbly     もう (kun'yomi) "mou"
平 - flat, peace     へい (on'yomi) "hei"
礼 - thanks     れい (on'yomi) "rei"
代 - substitue, replace, period, age     だい (on'yomi) "dai"

I'm hoping to speed up my progress in WaniKani. Learning a language isn't a race, but I've slowed down quite a bit on level four.
