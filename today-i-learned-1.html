Today I Learned - 1

I doubt I'll do this with everything, but I'd like to start recording some of the Japanese I learn, as I learn it. For today, that means kanji.

  • 氷 - ice, こおり (kun'yomi)
  • 他 - other, た (on'yomi)
  • 休 - rest, きゅう (on'yomi)
  • 先 - previous, せん (on'yomi)
  • 世 - world, せい (on'yomi)
  • 字 - letter, じ (on'yomi)
  • 主 - master, しゅ (on'yomi)
  • 年 - year, ねん (on'yomi)
  • 名 - name, めい (on'yomi)
  • 皿 - plate, さら (kun'yomi)
  • 写 - copy, しゃ (on'yomi)
  • 申 - say humbly, もう (kun'yomi)
  • 平 - flat, peace, へい (on'yomi)
  • 礼 - thanks, れい (on'yomi)
  • 代 - substitue, replace, period, age, だい (on'yomi)

I'm hoping to speed up my progress in WaniKani. Learning a language isn't a race, but I've slowed down quite a bit on level four.
