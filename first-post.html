一つ

I'm bad at first posts. Is there such a thing as a good "first post" to anything? Anyway...

 

I'm using this site to track my progress while I learn Japanese. It is unlikely that many other people will read it. But, my thought is that things will stick in my head more firmly if I write about them. So, here we are.

So far, I've learned:

  • Kana (hiragana and katakana).
    • I used a combination of an app on my phone and a game called "You Can Kana" for PC (highly recommended for complete beginners).
  • ~65 kanji.
    • Mostly using WaniKani.
  • ~125 vocabulary words.
    • Also mostly with WaniKani.
  • Grammar basics (just started) using:
    • Tae Kim's Grammar Guide
    • Genki I
    • Bunpro

I've also tried out a (zero) graded reader and, while having only 125 words/65 kanji under my belt means I still need to look up most of the words, I think it has been a useful exercise.
