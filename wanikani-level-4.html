WaniKani Level 4

I crossed into level four on WaniKani today. According to my stats, I've learned 80 radicals, 80 kanji, and 124 vocabulary words. I started with WaniKani 32 days ago. It's certainly not the fastest progression I've seen, but I'm very happy with where I'm at. Full stats:

I'm also pretty satisfied with my accuracy. Remembering the meaning of the radicals/kanji/vocab has (so far) been fairly easy. I've had to focus much harder on the readings. Counting things/people/days/months/etc. tripped me up quite a bit. Knowing when to use which reading of a number has been a bit of a struggle, but I finally feel like I'm getting it right almost every time.

There is a local chapter of the National Association fo Japan-America Societies in my city that offers Japanese language classes. They are (understandably) not inexpensive, but I may consider trying one at some point, if only for the conversation and listening practice I would gain.
